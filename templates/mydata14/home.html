Hello! and welcome,

Tournaments are going to be used in Physics 405 this semester to evaluate various aspects of your work. As a participants in these tournaments you will be asked to submit your work and make comparisons of other students work. All of the comparisons you make will be personally assigned to you by a computer program.

The basic idea is that through participation in this process you will see how others approach their work in physics and think critically about their work, which will help you will learn more. It will also make grading your hoemwork more efficient and improve the overall quality of grading. All of the tournament results will be available online as the tournaments complete so reviewing the outcomes and using this information to your benefit will be up to you.
